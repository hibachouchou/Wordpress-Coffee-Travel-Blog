Who doesn’t enjoy waking up to the smell of fresh coffee in the morning? And if you have a coffee maker chances are you use ground coffee.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

While many coffee makers consider switching to grinding their own beans, it definitely still holds true that most of us still resort to pre-ground coffee most of the time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

And when it comes to pre-ground, perhaps you got tired of that same brand you’ve been using for years now. Perhaps you could use a dose of fresh inspiration to try something new.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

That’s where this post comes in! We’ve researched tons of ground coffees on the market to bring you only the best. If you’re looking to try out something new, then this post is for you!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  1. Volcanica Coffee – Ethiopian Yirgacheffe<\/strong><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n\n

    This is a unique coffee that is harvested mainly from wild coffee trees. A medium roast with a sweet and complex flavor boasting hints of lemon and berry, this is one of the company’s best sellers by far – and we can see why.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    It is grown in the Yirgacheffe region in the south of Ehiopia, one that is well known for coffees toned with floral and berry tones. Volcanica is also fair trade and organic certified.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    They offer three different grind sizes: french press, drip or espresso. We highly recommend giving this one a shot!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    1. Lifeboost Light Roast Coffee<\/strong><\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n\n

      Lifeboost is a premium organic coffee brand.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Their light roast is a single origin hailing from the Nicaraguan Mountains, and is grown without use of pesticides, chemicals, GMOs or mycotoxins. Their coffee is also fair trade certified.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      This one has a mild clean test free of bitterness. Think hints of caramel chocolate topped by a light and fruity flavor. It is also gentle on the stomach due to its naturally low levels of acid.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      You won’t regret trying out this one if you like the idea of a fresh, organic, premium coffee!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
